EMMANUEL ONYEIKE - CEO

Based in Port Harcourt and a proud student of Godfrey Okoye University, a private university in Enugu State, Emmanuel Onyeike is a visionary Nigerian entrepreneur passionate about innovation in the tech space.

He began his tech journey in 2019 after a brief but inspiring stint at PXXL Space, a hosting company led by Honour Robinson Amadi. This experience sparked the birth of Prime Aztecs, launched in January 2022.

Emmanuel led Prime Aztecs as CEO until March 2024, when he went on a tech excursion to gain deeper knowledge.

Upon his return, he co-founded Tech-Nest with his team: Victor Chukwuemeka (COO) and Afeez Temitope (CTO). The trio transitioned the brand into TECH-NXT, officially launched on May 15th, 2025.

TECH-NXT is now a thriving innovation hub focused on uniting the tech community, fostering opportunities, and nurturing the next generation of developers.

The team is currently preparing for the launch of T Learning Pro, a powerful learning platform that teaches multiple programming languages and provides access to other transformative tech features.

VICTOR CHUKWUEMEKA - COO

Victor Chukwuemeka is a driven Nigerian tech entrepreneur and a proud student of Godfrey Okoye University, the same respected private institution attended by Emmanuel Onyeike.

He started his tech journey in September 2023, demonstrating a deep passion for software, innovation, and meaningful digital solutions.

In recognition of his strong leadership and collaborative mindset, he was invited to become COO of TECH-NXT under the visionary leadership of Emmanuel Onyeike (CEO) and alongside Afeez Timotope (CTO).

Victor plays a crucial role in managing day-to-day operations, building internal processes, and ensuring the steady growth and alignment of the TECH-NXT team with its mission.

With a clear vision and fierce determination, Victor Chukwuemeka continues to empower TECH-NXT to shape the future of tech in Nigeria and beyond.

OKEKE MARK CHUKWUEMEKA - MANAGER

Okeke Mark Chukwuemeka is a results-driven individual who brings structure and coordination to TECH-NXT as the Manager.

He studied at Godfrey Okoye University, the same institution as Emmanuel Onyeike and Victor Chukwuemeka.

Mark began his tech journey in September 2024, showing dedication and rapid growth.

After a productive sit-down with Emmanuel, his potential was recognized and he was officially invited to join TECH-NXT as Manager.

His proactive mindset, leadership skills, and team-first attitude keep TECH-NXT aligned with its mission of nurturing future tech leaders.
